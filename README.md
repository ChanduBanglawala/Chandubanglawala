
# 💫 About Me:
Hi, I'm Chandu Banglawala, a Data Science graduate and currently pursuing M.Sc. Data Science at Osmania University, where I secured Rank 3 in the CPGET entrance examination. My interests lie at the intersection of Statistics, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Generative AI, and I am passionate about leveraging data and AI to solve real-world problems.<br>Through academic projects and hands-on experience, I have worked on predictive modeling, healthcare analytics, and computer vision applications using technologies such as Python, TensorFlow, Scikit-learn, Pandas, and Streamlit. I have developed machine learning solutions for disease prediction and explored deep learning approaches for image classification and pattern recognition.<br>Beyond traditional machine learning, I am actively exploring Large Language Models (LLMs), AI Agents, Transformers, and Generative AI. Driven by curiosity and continuous learning, I enjoy transforming complex data into meaningful insights and building intelligent systems that create real-world impact. My goal is to contribute to innovative AI solutions that combine strong statistical foundations with modern machine learning techniques.
